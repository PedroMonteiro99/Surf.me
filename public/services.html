How does it work?

01

Create an Account

When creating an account an user can choose between smart or non smart Urban Farm

02

Setting the Urban Farm

Users can choose the type and quantity of sensors they want and even the culture they want to plant in their farm

03

Controlling the Urban Farm

The website will control your farm and send you alerts when in need
